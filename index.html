How The App Works?

1

Install & Signup

You sign up as an organisation, get a unique in your provided email, register an administrator, and register a classroom. You are now good to go and start using the iRegisterKids desktop apps.

2

Upload & Create

Upload a picture when you register as an administrator. Each admin istrator can upload their own picture too. You can either take your picture with the camera option or choose a saved picture on your device

3

Activate Device

You can activate every device connected to your account as an administrator. Note that each administrator has each permissions which can limit users/administrators.
